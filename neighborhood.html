My Neighborhood of Lawrenceville

I am from a suburb of Atlanta named Lawrenceville in Georgia. It is within the county of Gwinnett and has a population of approximately 30,516.

It contains many restaurants of various types that vary in average price.

Here are my top 5 favorite Restaurants

  1. Iron Age, a korean BBQ place
  2. Marlow's Tavern, an American food style restaurant
  3. Ted's Montana Grill, an American Steakhouse
  4. Provinos, an Italian Restaurant
  5. Frankie's The Steakhouse, another Steakhouse
